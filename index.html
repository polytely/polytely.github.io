Data Analytics Solutions for Energy Management and Environmental Sensing

Polytely aims to utilize modern (big or not) data analytics techniques to build solutions that support a diverse set of energy consumers, with varying capabilities, to become part of a community that collaborates and learns towards achieving local sufficiency through improved energy efficiency and deployment of micro-generation technologies.

Our goal is to develop the necessary infrastructure to perform scalable, adaptive learning of the consuming behaviour, efficiency improvements and generation capabilities of smart energy community members that collaborate to ensure quality of energy supply and environment preservation. The reason why we aim to explore the issue of citizen participation in the energy system transition by focusing on community formation is that while citizen behaviour in relation to energy consumption/prosumption and energy efficiency is affected by a series of interrelated factors, the creation and mobilization of communities around a shared goal, can affect a significant number of these factors.
