Polytely Analytics

Data Analytics Solutions for Energy Management and Environmental Sensing

Welcome to Polytely's web home

Polytely aims to utilize modern (big or not) data analytics techniques to build solutions to support a diverse set of energy consumers, with varying capabilities, to become part of a community that collaborates and learns towards achieving local sufficiency through improved energy efficiency and deployment of micro-generation technologies.

Polytely has been created with the goal of developing the necessary infrastructure to perform scalable, adaptive learning of the consuming behaviour, efficiency improvements and generation capabilities, if micro-generation is present, of "smart energy community" members that that collaborate to ensure quality of energy supply and environment preservation. The reason why we aim to explore the issue of citizen participation in the energy system transition focusing on community formation is that citizen behaviour in relation to energy consumption/prosumption and energy efficiency is affected by a series of interrelated factors, and the creation and mobilization of communities, the members of which collaborate to achieve a shared goal, can affect a significant number of these.

The central notion of our concept is that by adopting a data-driven approach, one can build a descriptive model of the consumers' behaviour, which can then be utilized for provision of guidance towards small, gradual and thus persistent changes in their consumption profiles.
